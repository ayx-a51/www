There is no test protocol – use the apps as often or as little as you like. Feedback is welcome via email (r@42.ai), in person, or by phone. Of particular interest are:

  • Does everything work smoothly or are there bugs?
  • Are texts correct and understandable (German/English)?
  • Is the operation intuitive or confusing?
  • Are processes logical or unexpected?

I will collect all feedback and carefully review it. Your input is valuable for future improvements!

Please keep the apps installed for at least 14 days so Google recognizes the tests as valid. Thank you for your understanding!
